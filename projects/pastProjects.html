Hybrid VR Experience
Project funded by UIC Honors College, with Dr. Mitchell Theys as project advisor. Developed VR application with Oculus Rift DK2 that recreated UIC's East Campus with 3D SketchUp mass models provided by the Office of Facility and Space planning. Optimized models with 3DS Max then piped into Unreal Engine 4 and later Unity for users to visualize East Campus (provides scale and direction).
(Fall 2015 - Spring 2016)

MIPS Assembler/CPU
Developed a MIPS assembler that supports instructions (written in C) and Data Path (designed in Verilog) to take MIPS assembly and feed it into my custom designed CPU model. In class presentation was done explaining the creation process, project was done under the guidance of Professor Paolo Vinella. (Fall 2016)
