how do i get my weather?
Google Voice Assistant
Me: "Ok Google, how's the weather?"
Google: "I'm sorry I didn't catch that."
Me: "Ah, I see that sounds great!"

Usually I just ask the Google Voice Assistant on my phone how the weather is in the morning as I frantically run to catch the train for my morning commute to Chicago. It's a convient method for me since it's almost hands free and I get a spoken response rather than just the information on the display. Having both is ideal since the Google responds saying both the temperature and probability of rain/snow during the day today. This in turn prompts me to grab my umberrala or jacket depending on the situation. However, with the relatively new home devices like Alexa and Google Home I might be changing my habits in the near future (i.e. poor university student becomes rich alumni)!
