Vijayraj Mahida
My name is Vijayraj Mahida and I am an undergraduate student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at the University of Illinois at Chicago (pursuing a B.S. in Computer Engineering) and an undergraduate research assistant at the Electronic Visualization Laboratory. I draw on a diverse set of skills, including designing and implementing: mechatronic systems, intelligent vehicles, robotics, video games, electronics, sensors, fabrication, and scientific visualization.

I enjoy working on interdisciplinary teams to design and implement meaningful technologies that help shape our world. Technology itself is most definitely a means and not an end. Strongly believe Einstein’s words, “The most beautiful and deepest experience a man can have is the sense of the mysterious.”
